JIRA Service Management

Enter your JIRA credentials to enable syncing new Organizations from JIRA Service Management into your local customer list. The Customers tab always shows your local list immediately — JIRA is only checked when you click Refresh on the Customers tab. Credentials are stored locally in your browser only.

Local Proxy (CORS fix)

Because this app runs as a local HTML file, browsers block direct calls to Atlassian. Run the included proxy server and point the JIRA Base URL here to it:

  1. Open Terminal
  2. Navigate to the ams-proxy folder
  3. Run: bash start-proxy.sh
  4. Come back here and set JIRA Base URL to http://localhost:3131
  5. Enter your real JIRA email and API token
  6. Click Save & Test

The proxy runs entirely on your machine — your credentials never leave it. Keep the Terminal window open while using the dashboard. The customer list is always available locally — JIRA is only contacted when you explicitly click Refresh on the Customers tab.
